# LaptopMatcher
https://studentlaptopmatcher.vercel.app/

LaptopMatcher is a personalized laptop recommendation platform built specifically for Indian college students.

Instead of showing generic bestseller lists or overwhelming spec sheets, LaptopMatcher recommends laptops based on:

* Academic major
* Budget
* Personal priorities
* Future-proofing requirements

Students answer a short quiz and receive ranked recommendations tailored to their needs.



## Recommendation Algorithm

The recommendation engine generates a score out of 10 for every laptop.

Formula:

- Major Fit (60%)
- Priority Alignment (40%)
- Value Bonus (up to +2)

The top-scoring laptops are ranked and presented as personalized recommendations.

This approach prioritizes academic suitability while still respecting individual preferences.



## Why LaptopMatcher?

Most laptop recommendation websites focus on gamers or general consumers.

LaptopMatcher is built specifically for Indian college students, helping them choose laptops based on how they will actually be used during their degree rather than simply comparing raw specifications.
